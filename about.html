Empowering Higher Education to Drive a Greener, Smarter Europe

GreenTech 4 Transformation (GT4T) unites universities, researchers, and entrepreneurs to turn deep-tech research into market-ready solutions for the circular economy, digital transformation, and clean energy. Through venture-science labs, challenge-based learning, and inclusive programmes for both young and experienced professionals, our 14-partner consortium will launch 30 start-ups, forge 60 strategic collaborations, and train over 1 000 participants—positioning Europe's HEIs as catalysts of a sustainable, innovation-driven economy.

Misyon

GT4T'nin misyonu, üniversitelerin ve araştırma kurumlarının döngüsel ekonomi, temiz enerji dönüşümü, dijital dönüşüm ve derin teknoloji inovasyonu yoluyla gerçek dünya sorunlarını çözmedeki rolünü güçlendirmektir.

Vizyon

Vizyonumuz; eğitim, teknoloji ve topluluk odaklı inovasyonun yeşil ve dijital dönüşüme öncülük ettiği, kapsayıcı ve dirençli bir Avrupa inşa etmektir.

Değerler

İş birliği, kapsayıcılık ve etki. GT4T, nesiller ve disiplinler arası çeşitliliği bir araya getirir, inovasyona adil erişimi destekler ve uzun vadeli toplumsal ve çevresel değer yaratmayı hedefler.

Why GT4T Stands Out

GT4T brings together emerging and experienced professionals to foster inclusive, cross-generational innovation. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures that every voice—from students to senior experts—contributes to solving real-world sustainability challenges. By bridging research, entrepreneurship, and policy, we create a dynamic ecosystem where impactful ideas thrive and collaboration drives meaningful change across Europe.

About GT4T

GreenTech 4 Transformation (GT4T) is a Europe-wide initiative empowering higher education institutions (HEIs) to become pioneers in sustainable innovation, entrepreneurship, and societal change. By focusing on critical areas like the circular economy, digital transformation, and energy transition, GT4T bridges research, education, and technology to address today's most urgent global challenges.

  • 30 new start-ups
  • 60 strategic partnerships
  • 3 market-ready deep-tech solutions

GT4T not only contributes to local and regional innovation goals but also strengthens Europe's position as a global leader in the green and digital transition. The project is supported by the EIT HEI Initiative under EIT Climate-KIC, the EU's leading climate innovation community.

About Our Supporters

GT4T is proudly supported by the EIT HEI Initiative, an EU-funded program under the European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT). The initiative aims to strengthen the innovation capacity of higher education institutions across Europe. Our project is guided and co-funded by EIT Climate-KIC, Europe's leading climate innovation community. EIT Climate-KIC supports impactful partnerships, deep-tech solutions, and sustainable transformation in education, business, and policy. Thanks to this strong backing, GT4T connects top institutions and partners across Finland, Germany, Ireland, Türkiye, and the Netherlands — building a united effort for a greener and more innovative Europe.
