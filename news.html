GT4T launches Europe-wide green innovation challenge for universities and startups in 2024

GreenTech 4 Transformation (GT4T) is launching a Europe-wide challenge to empower universities and startups to co-create sustainable solutions for the circular economy, digital transformation, and clean energy. The initiative will provide funding, mentorship, and access to a unique innovation ecosystem. Applications open in March 2024 and are open to all higher education institutions and early-stage startups across Europe.
